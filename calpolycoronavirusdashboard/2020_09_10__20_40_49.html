On-Campus COVID-19 Testing

Updated Sept. 10, 1 p.m.

CAMPUS TESTING UPDATE

Total tests by CHW in prior 24-hour period: 86
Total positive tests of on-campus student residents (since July 8, 2020): 4
New positive tests of on-campus student residents (last 24 hours): 1
Total positive tests of off-campus student residents (since July 8, 2020) 66

Total recovered cases for on-campus student residents (since Sept. 3): 0
New recovered cases for on-campus student residents (last 24 hours): 0
Currently hospitalized on-campus student residents: 0

Cal Poly provides COVID-19 testing through its Campus Health and Wellbeing only to currently enrolled students. These numbers reflect only positive cases identified directly through Campus Health and Wellbeing. Students can also access testing off campus.

The university does not offer testing to employees. Employees who test positive for COVID-19 are reflected in countywide and community-specific statistics (for their community of residence), available at the San Luis Obispo County Public Health Department webpage: https://www.emergencyslo.org/en/covid19.aspx.

 

ISOLATION

Total on-campus resident students currently in isolation: 4
New on-campus resident students in isolation (last 24 hours): 1
Remaining Isolation Beds Available: 171

Students who test positive for COVID-19 are placed in isolation in on-campus University Housing facilities and provided with direct dining, laundry and other support services. Per the CDC, an individual who has tested positive can leave isolation if it has been 10 days since their symptoms first appeared, they haven’t had a fever in at least 24 hours without the use of fever-reducing medications, and their symptoms have improved.

 

QUARANTINE

Total on-campus resident students currently in quarantine: 5
New on-campus resident students in quarantine (last 24 hours): 3

Students who have been exposed to someone with COVID-19 should self-quarantine for 14 days.

 

For more on the university’s Isolation and Quarantine procedures, visit: https://coronavirus.calpoly.edu/student-quarantine-and-isolation.
